Meeting Abstract:

Fourth joint meeting of Sillicon Valley Agile and BayXP.

As last time, this meeting will be run as an Open Space*. Each person should come with a topic they are interested in discussing. I will collect the topics, we'll vote on them and set the agenda for the rest of the evening.

Topic proposals should be on the theme of "there's an idea I'd like to try". Come prepared to tell a story about an idea you'd like to try. Elements will likely include:

  • What is the idea?
  • Why? What is the problem you're trying to solve or the benefit you hope to achieve?
  • Where did you hear about the idea?
  • Why haven't you tried it yet?

Ideas could be at any scale, from "I'd like to try writing my UI test-first" to "I'd like to try interviewing by audition" to "I'd like to try reorganizing from functional teams to project teams".

In the best case someone else will have experience with the idea and can offer some useful suggestions, and at the very least we'll all hear about some interesting ideas.

* We will roughly follow the Salt Lake Agile Roundtable protocol
